====== Hamath, Lebo-Hamath ======

===== Facts: =====

Hamath was a Hittite city in northern Syria, north of the land of Canaan. The name Lebo-Hamath probably refers to the same city. There was also a "land of Hamath" which was the region surrounding the city.

  * King Tou of Hamath congratulated King David and gave him gifts because he had defeated Tou's enemies.
  * Hamath was said to be one of Solomon's storehouse cities where provisions were kept.
  * The land of Hamath was where King Nebuchadnezzar of Babylon had King Zedekiah killed.
  * Pharaoh Necho of Egypt captured King Jehoahaz in the land of Hamath.
  * Some versions translate "Lebo-Hamath" as "entrance to Hamath" or "border of Hamath," which are also possible meanings.
